Unary operators operate on a single operand, that is, they perform an operation on only one value.
Binary operators operate on two operands, performing a specific operation between them. 

In JavaScript, common unary operators include:-
Unary Plus '(+)',
Unary Negation '(-)',
Increment '(++)' and Decrement '(--)'.

In JavaScript, common binary operators include:-
+, *, /, %, ==, ===, !=, !==, >, <, >= ,<= ,&& , ||.
